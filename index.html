About Giyani Science Centre

Science is for everyone and it starts here in Giyani

The Giyani Science Centre is dedicated to promoting science education and engagement by offering interactive resources, event information, and learning opportunities in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics).

Our Mission

To empower our community through accessible, exciting, and relevant scientific education that inspires future innovators.

Our Vision

To be the leading hub for STEM education in the region, nurturing the next generation of scientists, engineers, and problem-solvers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM for special programs.

How can schools arrange visits?

Schools can contact us via phone or email to schedule visits. We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance.

Are there fees for your programs?

Many of our programs are free or offered at minimal cost thanks to community partnerships and sponsorships.

Do you offer programs for adults?

Yes! We host evening workshops and community science events that are open to all ages.
