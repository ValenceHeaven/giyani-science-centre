About Giyani Science Centre

SCIENCE IS FOR EVERYONE AND IT STARTS HERE IN GIYANI

The Giyani Science Centre is a dynamic hub for science education, dedicated to making STEM learning accessible and exciting for everyone. We provide hands-on interactive exhibits and digital resources that transform abstract scientific concepts into engaging, tangible experiences suitable for all ages and learning styles.

As a community focal point, we host diverse events including workshops, science fairs, guest lectures, coding bootcamps, and competitions that bring people together to celebrate and explore science. Our comprehensive programs‚Äîfrom after-school activities to holiday camps and educator resources‚Äîcomplement formal education and create pathways for students to pursue STEM careers.

We are particularly committed to reaching underrepresented groups and breaking down barriers to science education. By fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and curiosity, the Giyani Science Centre aims to build a scientifically literate community prepared to understand and shape the future.

Our Mission

To empower our community through accessible, exciting, and relevant scientific education that inspires future innovators.

Our Vision

To be the leading hub for STEM education in the region, nurturing the next generation of scientists, engineers, and problem-solvers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Is there an admission fee?

General admission is free for all visitors. Some special workshops and events may have a small fee to cover materials.

Do you offer school group tours?

Yes! We welcome school groups and offer guided tours tailored to different grade levels. Please book at least two weeks in advance.

How can I volunteer at the centre?

We're always looking for passionate volunteers! Contact our volunteer coordinator or fill out the form on our website to get started.
