About Us

Empowering the next generation of scientists, engineers, and innovators in Giyani and beyond.

The Giyani Science Centre is dedicated to promoting science education and engagement by offering interactive resources, event information, and learning opportunities in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics).

Our mission is to make science accessible, exciting, and relevant to everyone in our community, fostering local pride in innovation and creating pathways to learning, careers, and discovery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Giyani Science Centre.

What are your operating hours? ‚àí

We are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM for special programs and events. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

For school visits and group bookings, we recommend scheduling in advance to ensure availability.

How can schools arrange visits to the Science Centre? +

Schools can book visits by contacting us via phone or email at least two weeks in advance. We offer curriculum-aligned programs for different grade levels and can accommodate groups of up to 50 students per session.

Our education team will work with you to create a customized experience that complements your classroom learning.

Are there programs for adults and families? +

Yes! We offer family science days, adult workshops, and community events throughout the year. Check our events calendar for upcoming activities suitable for all ages.

Our family programs are designed to be interactive and engaging for both children and adults, making learning a fun family experience.

Do you offer volunteering opportunities? +

We welcome volunteers with backgrounds in science, education, or simply a passion for sharing knowledge. Please contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities and requirements.

Volunteers can assist with workshops, events, mentoring, and various educational programs. Training is provided for all volunteer positions.

How can I support the Science Centre? +

There are several ways to support us: become a corporate sponsor, make a donation, volunteer your time, or help spread the word about our programs in your community. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

Your support helps us reach more students and expand our educational programs throughout the Giyani region.

What safety measures are in place for visitors? +

We prioritize the safety of all our visitors. Our facilities are equipped with safety equipment, and all activities are supervised by trained staff. We conduct regular safety drills and maintain all equipment to the highest standards.

For school groups, we require teacher supervision and provide safety briefings before all activities.
