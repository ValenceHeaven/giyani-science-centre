About Giyani Science Centre

Discover our mission to make STEM education accessible and exciting for everyone

SCIENCE IS FOR EVERYONE AND IT STARTS HERE IN GIYANI

The Giyani Science Centre is a dynamic hub for science education, dedicated to making STEM learning accessible and exciting for everyone. We provide hands-on interactive exhibits and digital resources that transform abstract scientific concepts into engaging, tangible experiences suitable for all ages and learning styles.

As a community focal point, we host diverse events including workshops, science fairs, guest lectures, coding bootcamps, and competitions that bring people together to celebrate and explore science. We are particularly committed to reaching underrepresented groups and breaking down barriers to science education. By fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and curiosity, the Giyani Science Centre aims to build a scientifically literate community prepared to understand and shape the future.

Our Mission

To empower our community through accessible, exciting, and relevant scientific education that inspires future innovators.

Our Vision

To be the leading hub for STEM education in the region, nurturing the next generation of scientists, engineers, and problem-solvers.

Our History

From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2018, the Giyani Science Centre began as a community initiative to address the growing need for accessible STEM education in the Limpopo region. What started as a small outreach program has grown into a state-of-the-art facility serving thousands of students and community members annually.

Through partnerships with local schools, government agencies, and corporate sponsors, we've expanded our offerings to include cutting-edge technology, interactive exhibits, and comprehensive educational programs that inspire the next generation of scientists and innovators.

Our journey continues as we work towards our vision of making quality science education available to every child in our community, regardless of their background or circumstances.
